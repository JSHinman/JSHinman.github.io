Vex robotics is a competition for high schools. This involves designing, building, coding and driving robots. Each year there is a new game, where two robots on each team try to get the highest score. Last year the game was Change-up, a game where you had to score balls is small hoops as fast as possible. Robotics at CBHS is held after school everyday until around 4:30pm in F10.
